Chocolate fudge recipe

This recipe results in a delicious chocolate fudge. It makes about 60 servings. The recipe calls for very few ingredients that can be easily be found at your local grocery store.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk

Directions:

After acquiring all of the ingredients, see to the directions as follows:

  1. Grease an 8x8 inch square baking pan. Set aside.
  2. Combine sugar, cocoa and milk in a medium saucepan. Stir to blend, then bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer. Do not stir again.
  3. Cook until temperature reaches about 240 degrees F.
  4. Remove from heat. Add butter or margarine and vanilla extract. Stir with a spoon until the fudge thickens. Do not under stir.
  5. Pour into pan and let cool. Cut into about 60 squares.
